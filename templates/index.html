Rules

Objective:

The objective of the game is to score points by hitting the ball past your opponent's paddle and into their side of the screen.

Controls:

  • Player 1 (Left Side):
    • Move up: Press W
    • Move down: Press S
  • Player 2 (Right Side):
    • Move up: Use the Up Arrow
    • Move down: Use the Down Arrow

Game Start:

The game begins with the ball in the center of the screen. A player will serve the ball by pressing a designated "Serve" button, or the game may start automatically.

Ball Movement:

The ball bounces off the top and bottom walls. If the ball hits either the left or right wall, it will reset to the center, and the opponent will score a point.

Paddles:

Each player controls a vertical paddle that can be moved up or down to intercept the ball.

Scoring:

A player scores when the ball crosses the opponent's side of the screen. The score is displayed at the top of the screen.
